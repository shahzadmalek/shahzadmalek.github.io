JunctionX

Society of Nerds

JunctionX is a society that is created within North-West University, Mafikeng Campus by the Khangwelo Magada(Founder) which aims to educate and provide basic skillsets to Information Systems, Computer Science or any students who are interested in learning about growing technologies, developing websites or applications that can help them find jobs or make a living by using this skillset to generate income for themselves

Mission, Vision & Benefits of JunctionX

Mission, Vision & Goals

  • Vision: Transformative and innovative technology education and training.
  • Mission: To streamline and accelerate skill development and training to students and business owners.
  • Goal:To teach basic skillsets to enhance individuals who have desire to learn in the fields of Information Technology, Computer Science and Information Systems such as in web-development, database, IoT devices, and application development that can help individuals get jobs easily or make a living by using this skillsets to their advantages to make a living for themselves
  • Services: Transformative technology workshops, data analysis, business intelligence and student financial literacy workshops

Benefits

The benefits of JunctionX programmes is that JunctionX helps students:

  • Learn more about the fields of IT that can help them get direction on a specific career they wish to choose
  • Gain knowledge and experience on certain tools and techniques which they can use to develop their projects
  • Find employment
  • And lastly, use this skillset to make a living for themselves

What have I learned

Personally I, as member and part of Junctionx programme, has learned alot from this experience and being a part of being in this programme. The things I learned are:

  • To work in a team of members who have skills and knowledge in various fields of IT.
  • To share the knowledge and skills I have with other members and students.
  • To act as a mentor to guide and help students who have no past experience or knowledge in IT fields learn about various topics e.g. web-development
  • learn about various tools and techniques that can be used to develop interactive UI/UX web applications or programmes
